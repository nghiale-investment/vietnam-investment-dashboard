Sector Rotation

Sector view: 10d share is each sector's traded value over the latest 10 trading days divided by total coverage traded value. Prior share uses the preceding 40 trading days.

Rotation delta: 10d share minus prior 40d share. Bars are ranked from the largest positive delta to the weakest delta.

Selected sector or custom basket: the chart switches to stock value surge, calculated as 10d average traded value divided by prior 40d average traded value, then shown as percentage above or below baseline.

Sector Table

Score scale: each input is percentile-ranked across the coverage universe at the selected base date. The stock score is the average of the available percentile ranks, from 0.00 to 1.00.

Current 20-day method: trend proximity, 5d-vs-prior-20d turnover surge, 10d adjusted price momentum, 5d-vs-prior-20d value surge, and sector rotation delta.

Filters: sector clicks or ticker search only change which stocks are displayed. The percentile score is still calculated against the full coverage universe.
